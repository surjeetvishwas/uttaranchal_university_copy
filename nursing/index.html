About Uttaranchal
College of Nursing

Uttaranchal College of Nursing, a constituent institute of Uttaranchal University was established in the year 2021 with an aim to prepare highly knowledgeable and more competent fraternities in the field of Nursing. The institution is committed to create and groom the students to deal with the demanding challenges in the health care system at the National and International levels.

The institution strives to inculcate the best possible evidence-based practices in the areas of nursing education, practice, administration, and research through outcome-based education. The institution provides a wide range of opportunities to the budding nurses to promote all-round development in the various domains of education and clinical practice by adopting prescribed standards at the national and global levels.

The institution is approved by the Government of Uttarakhand, Uttarakhand Nurses and Midwives council as well as Indian Nursing Council, New Delhi.

Vision & Mission

Vision

  • To be a leading and outstanding health care institution through applying innovative concepts in nursing education, clinical practice and research with an aim to have a positive impact on humankind.

Mission

  • To create an eco-environment by which students may reach their full potential in the field of nursing education and to be an excellent nurse.
  • To ensure individualised care to the multicultural society through therapeutic interactions.
  • To provide an evidence-based learning experiences through rigorous research actions in order to meet the demanding challenges of the health care system. 
  • To promote all-round personality development through in-depth technical knowledge and application of various skills in practice.
  • To emphasize on ethical and moral principles through an appropriate professional conduct, dignity and honest.
