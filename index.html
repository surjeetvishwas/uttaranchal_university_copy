Uttaranchal University Dehradun
For the Excellence within "U"

Uttaranchal University is recognized by the UGC under sections 2(f) and 12 (B) of the UGC Act, 1956, and by other statutory bodies of the State and the Nation. The University has been promoted by Sushila Devi Centre for Professional Studies & Research, a society registered under the Societies Registration Act (1860). The University has been accredited with the prestigious “A+ Grade” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Uttaranchal University has become the first university in the State of Uttarakhand to receive "NAAC A+" Grade in the first cycle of Accreditation and thus created a history.

Best Infrastructure

Our unmatched infrastructure functions as a ladder to our student's success and makes them future-ready.

Grand Libraries

Multiple libraries stocking lakhs of books and e-books catering to different colleges inside the campus. The Central Library is the soul of Uttaranchal University with a carpet area of 2,430 Sqm. It holds a hybrid collection of printed as well electronic resources, which include books, journals, theses dissertations databases, audio-visuals, CDs/DVDs, e-books, e-journals, reports, case studies, conference proceedings, training manuals pertaining to all subjects under various courses.

Know More

World-class Computer Centre

The centre supports an institute-wide 1,000 Mbps fibre optic network that connects all the academic departments, hostels, libraries, other blocks, and other central facilities to this centre. It has a computer system with servers (Linux server, domain server, database server, Thin Client Server, backup server, ERP server and storage).

The e-campus comprises internet, which remains open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year and has been connected with a 1 GBPS which offers high speed and uninterrupted connectivity.

Know More

Auditorium

Uttaranchal University houses Swami Vivekananda Auditorium with a seating capacity of 1,200; it also incorporates other state-of-the-art features.

Know More

Home-like Hostels

At Uttaranchal University, we offer on-campus residence facilities to students and have separate hostel buildings forgirls and boys. The hostels are equipped with all the facilities to provide a comfortable stay to students. There is provision for single room occupancy, double room occupancy, three-room occupancy, and four-room occupancy. The rooms of our hostels are well-furnished and properly lighted. Each room is furnished with a bed, mattress, study table, chair, and cupboard.

Know More

Spacious Dining

Great care is taken to create a home-like atmosphere by providing nutritious meals cooked in an utmost hygienic way in modern cooking ware. The mess staff takes special care to plan out a weekly menu that satisfies the vegetarian and non-vegetarian tastes. There is a separate mess for boys and girls.

Know More

Medical Facilities

University has tie-ups with the best hospitals in the district, including Max Healthcare and Shri Mahant Indiresh Hospital, to provide immediate medical attention to the ailing students. The university has a dedicated ambulance van, which is available 24x7 for all 365 days, to take any sick student to the hospital. First aid services are available on the university campus itself.

Know More

Cafeteria

The University has also set up two eateries, which provide delicious muffins, sandwiches, different flavours of coffee, soft drinks, and piping hot snacks. No effort is spared to offer the best food to students. The joints have comfortable seating arrangements and provide respite to the students after their gruelling academic schedule.

Know More

Food Court

Food Court, with a seating capacity of 500 students at a time, offers a great multi-cuisine spread, including South Indian, Chinese and more, to cater to the taste buds of young boys and girls from different parts of the country. The meals offered by the Food Court, the ambiance of this place, and the swarms of students hovering around, lend a great vibrancy to campus life. In the evening, this place is abuzz with life and laughter.

Know More

Transport

The University provides transport facility to its day scholars on nominal charges through its self-owned luxurious buses. The buses ply on different routes of the city to pick and drop the students from various areas. There are separate buses plying for the staff.
