ABOUT US

WHY UTTARANCHAL
UNIVERSITY?

Uttaranchal University has been recognized by the UGC under sections 2(f) and 12 (B) of the UGC Act, 1956, and by other statutory bodies of the State and the Nation.The University has been accredited with the prestigious “A+ Grade” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Uttaranchal University has become the first University in the State of Uttarakhand to receive NAAC A+ Grade in the first cycle of Accreditation, thus creating a history.

From excellent teaching to accessing amazing placement opportunities; Uttaranchal University has it all. Meet our inspiring academic staff, to help you thrive, and find out about some of the incredible placement opportunities our students take on to shape their future careers.

Academic Excellence

We attract the best minds towards teaching and research so that our graduates are a force to reckon within the new interconnected and border- less world of the new millennium. Our faculty members inspire students to develop holistically for a meaningful life.

Industry Experience

Placements are a key part of courses at Uttaranchal University. We know how much employers value graduates who have real-world experience, and that is why we encourage our students to make the most of the opportunities available.

WHY CHOOSE INDIA?

The Indian educational system is the second largest in the world, and it is well-structured and rigorous,
producing some of the world’s most skilled students. It's no wonder that India is often recognized as one
of the most popular destinations in the world.

Courses Abound

India's higher education system is extensive not only in terms of size, but also in terms of academic offerings. Because of the country's rich history and thriving future, a wide range of courses, from the traditional to the cutting edge, are accessible.

Economy On-Rise

India's economy continues to grow at a rapid pace. It is not only anticipated to be the world's third largest economy by 2030, but also to grow at the highest rate in the decade ahead, according to experts.

Campus Life

The best part of campus life in India is that the majority of the country's universities are residential, allowing international students to live in a safe and close-knit community. One of the most compelling reasons to study in India is the abundance of opportunities for international students to interact and socialise in extracurricular activities.

work
opportunities

While the country provides a wide range of programs in a variety of subjects, many international students travel to India in search of career possibilities both during and after their studies. The Indian government forbids international students from working throughout their studies. International students, on the other hand, are permitted to participate in training and internships. Even though, India bounds international students from exploring work opportunities during their studies, it generally does not exempt them from working in the country post program. However, students cannot extend their stay in India and work on their student visa. The Indian student visa does not carry any work related right.

Nevertheless, international students can work in the country under the employment visa scheme.

To be eligible to work in India after completing your studies, you must go through the immigration office and have a job offer from a registered company. International students can work in India for a particular period, after which their visa can be extended.
