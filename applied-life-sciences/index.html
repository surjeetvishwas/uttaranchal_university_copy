About School of Applied and Life Sciences

School of Applied and Life Sciences (SALS) aims to provide quality education encompassing the varied fields of science. The Institute is committed to the goal of preparing a skilled workforce and be at the zenith amongst leading Applied Science Institutes in the country.

Highly qualified and experienced faculty is the prized asset of the School of Applied and Life Sciences. Laboratories and practicals conducted are updated to keep abreast with the changing higher education scenario. The state-of-art infrastructure supplements our academic endeavours. The college has a well-conceptualised and planned layout. The Centrally operated Library provides books and internet facilities to the students. Modern well-equipped laboratories help in meeting the practical demands of the students.

The curriculum of B.Sc. and M.Sc. programmes are designed as per the latest guidelines of UGC and follow a choice-based credit system. Presentations, theoretical & practical exposure to Computational Skills, English Communication, participation in extracurricular activities, and lectures on human ethics are additional advantages facilitating the overall grooming of students.

SALS focuses on the placement of students which is visible through our various efforts aiming towards providing exposure. We regularly organize Industrial and Educational Tours, Seminars, and Workshops to facilitate exposure to the latest techniques and processes being used.

Applied & Life Science Programs

This interdisciplinary Applied & Life Sciences Programme offers a unique blend of courses from Biology, Chemistry, Biotechnology and Physics etc. Emphasis is placed on hands-on application of modern theories through laboratory courses and research opportunities. The programs are designed to prepare for careers in the Health Sector, Biotechnology Industry, Food Industry, Chemical Industry, and Diagnostic Laboratories.

Why School of Applied and Life Sciences?

The University has been accredited with the prestigious “A+ Grade” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Uttaranchal University has become the first and only private University in the State of Uttarakhand to receive NAAC A+ Grade in the first cycle of Accreditation and this firmly positions us among the top 5% HEIs of the nation.
