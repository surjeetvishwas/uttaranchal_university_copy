About Uttaranchal
Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences

Uttaranchal Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences (UIPS), established within the vast, clean, and green campus of Uttaranchal University, has become a recognized brand in education and research in Pharmaceutical Sciences. UIPS focuses on excellence in teaching and research. It strives to prepare students to become Pharmacists and Pharmaceutical Scientists who will be leaders in the pharmacy profession. At UIPS, we offer professional pharmacy education from basic to doctoral level with a focus on building a strong foundation for our students.

Besides being modern, the teaching system employed here is innovative and application-oriented. Additionally, we have well-equipped laboratories, highly qualified and experienced faculty, and supporting staff. Practical exposure is ensured by training during summer/winter vacation in Community/Hospital for the First year and Second year students, and Industrial Training & Research projects for the Third/Final Year Diploma/Degree students.

The students are encouraged to do open-ended laboratory-oriented and study-oriented projects at the degree level. Seminars, guest lectures, and workshops by eminent speakers from industry, hospitals, research institutes, and educational trips & industrial visits expose students to the basic and advanced concepts of pharmacy education.

Note – Programmes are approved by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi.

Uttaranchal Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences is a pioneer in training the next generation of Drug Control administrators, Food and Drug administrators, Drug dispensers, Chemical Analysts, and Hospital Managers, among others.

Hi-Tech Infrastructure at Uttaranchal Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences

Uttaranchal Institute of Pharmaceutical Sciences boasts excellent learning outcomes owing to its world-class laboratories, equipment, and museum.

Central Instrumentation Laboratory

The Central Instrumentation Laboratory is established for students, research scholars, and faculty members of the university who are actively engaged in R&D activities in Pharmaceutical Sciences. The Central Instrumentation Laboratory has sophisticated instruments like High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), UV-VIS Spectrophotometer, Flame Photometer, Calorimeter, Lyophilizer, and Sonicator.

Machine Room

The Machine Room is equipped with modern equipment and machinery used for pilot projects, formulation, and development studies in injectables, tablets, capsules, liquid orals, and ointment sections. It has a well-built area of machines, generally encountered in Pharmaceutical Industry, and provides a window for young and aspiring pharmacists to be well-versed in the current pharmaceutical scenario.

Pharmaceutics Laboratory

Pharmaceutics is concerned with the scientific and technological aspects of the design and manufacture of dosage forms. The laboratory is designed for providing instructions in several pharmaceutical technology aspects, particularly the manufacturing of various pharmaceutical dosage forms. It is equipped with the required machines and apparatus.

Human, Anatomy and Physiology Laboratory

The anatomy and Physiology laboratory is designed to provide a foundation in human biology. This includes the knowledge of the basic anatomical and histological organization of the human body and its physiology. Here, students learn how the various organs of the body interact with one another and contribute to the overall physiology of the body.

Pharmacognosy Laboratory

Pharmacognosy is defined as the study of physical, chemical, biochemical, and biological properties of drugs or potential drugs or drug substances of natural origin as well as the search for new drugs from natural sources. It’s a specialized area of applied science dealing with natural products that affect the health of humans and animals. This laboratory consists of organized as well as unorganized crude drugs. The lab provides guidelines to students regarding the natural source of drugs and phytochemical screening through various instruments. The laboratory is utilized for morphological and anatomy study of drugs of natural origin. The lab is associated with a museum in which herbal drugs are displayed.

Computer Laboratory

Computer Laboratory is designed to provide a teaching environment that develops specific skills in students and helps them master the technical skills needed in their particular field, as well as learn how software assists in information assimilation, analysis, and organization of material for presentation and effective decision-making. The Computer Laboratory offers different software applications to support varied program areas. This lab is equipped with the following software - Windows OS, MS Office, Powerpoint, Chem Draw, Chem Sketch, Ex -Pharm (Pharmacology Interactive Animal Model).

Museum

The museum has an extensive collection of different drugs (Photos) and displays dosage forms like tablets, capsules, creams, liniments, lozenges, syrups, first-aid kits, surgical instruments, and various medication containers.
