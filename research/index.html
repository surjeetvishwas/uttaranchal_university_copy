Research & Innovation Overview

Uttaranchal University is a research driven university with a commitment to research collaboration and impactful research. A Division of Research and Innovation has been established at Uttaranchal University with the latest infrastructure and eco-system for research, knowledge creation, dissemination and transdisciplinary innovations.

Research area

The university has mapped the research areas as per local, national, and international emerging area of Agriculture, Biotechnology, Security Information and Communication Technology, Environment and Climate Change, Industry 4.0, Sustainable Energy and Green Buildings, Public Healthcare, Nanotechnology and Advanced Materials. The research is focused on providing solutions to societal problems aligning with the United Nation’s agenda for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

We at the Division of Research & Innovation, Uttaranchal University are on a mission of finding Excellence within ‘U’ by providing infrastructure to support research, dissemination, and transdisciplinary innovations.

We at the Division of Research & Innovation, Uttaranchal University are on a mission of finding Excellence within ‘U’ by providing infrastructure to support research, dissemination, and transdisciplinary innovations.

We are looking forward to the solutions to the societal problems aligning the Industry 4.0 vision by adopting the United Nations agenda for Sustainable Development Goals. Industry 4.0 has been defined by the implementation of cyber physical systems for the automation in manufacturing, cloud computing and data exchange over Internet of Things. Our motto is to implement Industry 4.0 in different domains of studies including Law, Liberal Arts, Sciences, Agriculture, Pharmacy and Management.

Team research & innovation has focused on research ethics and teamwork for better society. We have the best research instrumentation facilities which can be availed by our faculty and also open for the external researcher & industry.

To promote research & innovation among faculty and students we have a research policy with provision of research leaves, bonus points and incentives on the basis of impact of research. We have an IPR cell and policy for promoting and filing patents by faculty/students. We appreciate “out of the box” thinking and look forward to creative thinkers to collaborate and participate in achieving the vision and mission goals of the University with global impact.

“Today is an Opportunity to build the tomorrow you want”

Division of Research & Innovation, Uttaranchal University provides the opportunity to build positive attitude for quality performance. Division has a vision to create an ecosystem for research & innovation for young minds. We work on the philosophy of creative thinking and learning.

Division has advanced central instrumentation laboratory with sophisticated instruments like XRD, FTIR, SEM under the supervision of well-trained faculty. We have “Centre of excellence in smart devices and IoT applications” to support prototyping to TRL6.

Faculty can avail provision of seed money for their research work with a motive of financial support to the potential researchers. We have research & innovation policies for ensuring research ethics and promoting research activities in the University. A separate IPR cell is to support & promote filing intellectual property rights.

Our focus is to be a research driven University and we believe it through participation in research and creative endeavours, an individual can become a better thinker, innovator, and problem solver.

We encourage faculty & students for transdisciplinary research and cultivate a depth of understanding in the students to make a positive difference in the society for a global impact.

We are open for collaboration with academia and Industry and looking forward for young and enthusiastic minds to join us.

Research Policy

To inculcate the culture of Research and Innovation among students and faculty members, a research policy has been implemented having the following salient features:

  • Seed money to faculty members for nurturing new research ideas.
  • Consultancy projects policy
  • Incentives and felicitation for Quality Research Publications
  • Grants for Patents/Copyrights/Trade Marks and Designs
  • Travel grants to faculty to attend conferences, present papers, and post-doctoral programs
  • Fellowships to research scholars
  • Conferences/Workshops/Seminars on Recent Trends and Technology
  • Summer Internships
  • Central Instruments Facilities

About

The Uttaranchal University has been recognized by UGC under section 2(f) and 12 (B) of the UGC Act, 1956 and other statutory bodies of the State and the Nation. The University has been accredited with the prestigious “A+ Grade” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Uttaranchal University has become the first and only private University in the State of Uttarakhand to receive NAAC A+ Grade in the first cycle of Accreditation and this firmly positions us among the top 5% HEIs of the nation.
